Our Story

Sarah Salahia's journey with Saromé began with love and curiosity. What started small, as a personal exploration of scents and emotions, blossomed into a passion for bottling feelings into fragrances.

"I created Saromé to share my vision of perfumery as an art form. Each scent is carefully composed like a symphony, with top notes that greet you, heart notes that reveal the character, and base notes that linger in memory."

Today, Saromé perfumes are cherished by those who seek more than just a scent - they seek a bottled emotion, a memory, a moment captured in fragrance.

Our Philosophy

At Saromé, we believe that a fragrance should be more than just a pleasant scent—it should be an experience, a memory, a statement.

We use only the finest ingredients sourced from ethical suppliers around the world. Each perfume is crafted in small batches to ensure quality and attention to detail.

Our perfumes are designed to evolve with you throughout the day, revealing different notes as time passes, just like a beautiful story unfolding.

Meet the Founder

Sarah Salahia

Founder & Perfume Creator

"I started small, with love and curiosity. Now, I bottle feelings into fragrances. Each Saromé scent is a journey - from the first spark of inspiration to the final blend that captures an emotion perfectly."

Sarah's approach to perfumery comes from the heart, blending techniques with personal intuition. Each fragrance tells a story, evokes a memory, or captures a feeling - because to Sarah, perfume is emotion made tangible.
